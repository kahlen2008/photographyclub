Our External Instructors

Ms. Clara Harris

Clara specialises in documentary and street photography. She guides students in visual storytelling, encouraging emotion-driven compositions and creative exploration. She also curates monthly photo showcases.

Mr. Alex Cavill

Alex teaches studio lighting, professional portraiture, and editing techniques. He’s known for helping members build competition-ready portfolios and confidence in directing shoots. His mentorship has led members to win regional awards.

Ms. Melanie Lee

Melanie brings her passion for nature and wildlife into the classroom. She leads sessions on macro and environmental photography, helping students appreciate patience, lighting, and the storytelling power of nature.
